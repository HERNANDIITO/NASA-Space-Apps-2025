Do you know what space weather is?

Actually, the Sun is like a big swimming pool where protons, electrons, and neutrons dance and play.

Our friends the electrons are very fast and love to move around,

the protons are full of energy and always want to party,

while the neutrons are very calm.

Sometimes the particles throw a party

in the pool where they sing and dance.

And sometimes the party gets out of control and the sun starts to get dizzy from all the movement.

When this happens, the sun gets really angry and ends the party in one go.
